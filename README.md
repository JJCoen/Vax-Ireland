# Vax-Ireland
Data gathering and investigation on vaccinations in Ireland on behalf of Humanity Projects

- Regulatory institutions are captured by financial and political interests
- They are unwilling or unable to get to the truth of the issues they set out to investigate
- Humanity Projects fulfills the need for unbiased and comprehensive research
- Enables policy decisions based on evidence
- Humanity Projects are independent agents who act as gatekeepers of the public interest.
- Provide high-quality research to other individuals and institutions.
